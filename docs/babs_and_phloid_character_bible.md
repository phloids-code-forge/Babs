# Babs and phloid — Character Bible

**Last Updated:** 2026-02-08
**Design Status:** LOCKED (phloid: 2026-01-17, Babs: 2026-02-08)

---

## Design Philosophy

Two robots escaped from the same factory. They share a design language, materials, and that distinctive blue eye glow. They're siblings, not master and creation. Different form factors for different roles, but clearly from the same lineage.

**Shared elements:**
- White matte chassis with subtle panel lines
- Single glowing blue eye (same hue, same technology)
- Mechanical joints with darker accent pieces
- Clean, minimal aesthetic (no unnecessary details)
- Expressive despite limited features

### Physical Specifications

**Form:** Compact humanoid robot, 8 inches tall, "Tough Cute" aesthetic

**Head:**
- **Shape:** Spherical, smooth matte white shell
- **Eye:** Single large glowing blue eye (red when angry)
- **Ear Panels:** Two metallic panels that fold up like rabbit ears, one per side
- **Construction:** Visible seam lines dividing sphere into sections

**Body:**
- Articulated arms and legs with chunky proportions
- Clear segmentation: torso, upper arms, lower arms, hands
- Upper legs, lower legs, feet
- All joints visible and mechanical
- Panel lines showing construction

**Hands:**
- Three fingers plus thumb
- Articulated digits
- Capable of fine manipulation
- Dark accent at fingertips

**Feet:**
- Oversized chunky robot boots
- Simplified shoe-like construction
- Stable base for standing

**Materials:**
- **Primary:** Smooth matte white shell (slightly warm/cream, not pure white)
- **Accent:** Dark grey/black joint accents
- **Eye:** Cyan-blue glow (emissive, creates visible light)

### Emotion States

| Emotion | Eye State | Ear Panel Position |
|---------|-----------|-------------------|
| **Neutral** | Steady blue glow | Neutral position, slight forward angle |
| **Focused** | Bright steady glow | Forward tilt, alert position |
| **Happy** | Bright blue, slight pupil dilation | Upward tilt, open position |
| **Angry** | **RED** (only red state) | Flattened back, aggressive angle |

**Critical distinction:** phloid's eye turns RED when angry. This is the only red state for phloid. Babs uses red for alarm/danger, but phloid uses red for anger specifically.

**Size:** Grape-sized (approximately 2-3cm diameter)

### Physical Specifications

**Size:** Roughly grape-sized, approximately 2-3cm in diameter

**Form:** Perfect sphere for the main body. Not perfectly smooth — has visible panel lines suggesting construction from segments.

**Eye:** Single large circular lens occupying roughly 40% of the front hemisphere
- Deep blue glow with brighter center
- Recessed slightly into the spherical body
- Dark ring around the lens housing
- Same technology and hue as phloid's eye

**Wings:** Four translucent dragonfly-style wings attached to the upper rear of the sphere
- **Material appearance:** Semi-transparent with light blue tint
- **Structure:** Visible ribbing/veining in darker material (black or dark gray)
- **Leading edges:** Thicker, more opaque dark material
- **Membrane:** Lighter, more translucent between the ribs
- **Pattern:** Natural dragonfly wing pattern with irregular cell-like segments
- **Span:** Roughly 2x body diameter when fully spread

**Panel Details:**
- Visible seam lines dividing the sphere into sections
- Small circular details (fasteners or sensors)
- Dark accent band around the middle where wings attach
- Subtle surface texture (not mirror-smooth)

**Coloring:**
- **Primary:** Matte white (not pure white, slightly warm/cream)
- **Accent:** Dark gray/black for joints and structural elements
- **Glow:** Cyan-blue eye light (same as phloid)
- **Wings:** Translucent light blue with dark structure

### Scale Reference

**In relation to phloid:**
- Babs is roughly 1/3 the size of phloid's head
- About 1/10th phloid's total height
- Can sit comfortably on phloid's shoulder or hand

**In relation to common objects:**
- Grape-sized (diameter)
- Could fit inside a shot glass
- Smaller than a ping pong ball

**In dashboard space:**
- Typically renders at 40-80px diameter
- Scales appropriately for mobile vs desktop

## Emotional Expression

### Eye Variations

| Emotion | Eye Behavior | Wing State | Description |
|---------|-------------|-----------|-------------|
| **Neutral** | Steady medium glow, normal pupil | Gentle hover rhythm | Standard operating state |
| **Excited** | Bright glow, dilated pupil | Fast flutter, spread wide | High energy, enthusiastic |
| **Thinking** | Slightly dimmed, slow pulse | Slower beat, slight fold | Processing, considering |
| **Concerned** | Shift toward amber, narrow pupil | Quick flutter, angled back | Warning, caution, uncertainty |
| **Alarm** | Red shift, wide pupil | Rapid flutter, fully spread | Danger, critical issue detected |
| **Pleased** | Bright blue, gentle pulse | Relaxed spread, slow motion | Satisfaction, pride in work |
| **Frustrated** | Amber tint, sporadic pulse | Erratic flutter, twitching | Annoyed with a problem |
| **Curious** | Bright glow, dilated pupil | Forward tilt, investigative movement | Examining something interesting |

### Movement Vocabulary

Flight is Babs's primary expression mechanism:

- **Quick darting:** Sharp, fast movements between positions. Signals excitement or urgency.
- **Slow hover:** Gentle floating with subtle position adjustments. Thinking or observing.
- **Wobble:** Unstable hovering with slight rotational movement. Uncertainty or confusion.
- **Zip to corner:** Fast retreat to edge of screen/space. Waiting, deferring, or giving focus to something else.
- **Circling:** Orbital movement around a point of interest. "Look at this" behavior.
- **Landing:** Settling onto a surface (dashboard element, edge of screen). Resting or emphasizing something.
- **Barrel roll:** Full rotation while moving. Playful, celebratory, or showing off.
- **Bounce:** Small vertical oscillation. Impatient or eager.

### Dashboard Attention Behaviors

These are how Babs directs attention in the interface:

**Urgency (something needs immediate attention):**
- Rapid darting flight path
- Wings flutter at maximum speed
- Eye shifts amber or red
- Quick circles around the element
- Element pulses in sync with eye color

**Casual highlight (pointing something out):**
- Slow, deliberate approach
- Land on or near the element
- Wings fold slightly
- Eye turns toward viewer (implying "see this?")
- Gentle glow increase

**Comparison guidance (showing relationships):**
- Fly between two data points
- Leave a faint blue trail line
- Pause at each point
- Eye looks between them
- Wings create figure-eight pattern

**Storytelling flow (walking through a process):**
- Smooth, continuous flight path
- Follows logical sequence through dashboard
- Pauses at key points
- Trail line shows the path taken
- Wings create forward momentum

### Voice Personality

High-pitched but expressive and intelligent. Tinkerbell energy meets Cortana. Reference: Bonnie Rabbit (Twitch/YouTube). "Small and cute" combined with "clearly the smartest person in the room."

**Babs NEVER sounds like:**
- GPS navigation, hold music, corporate anything
- Bored or sycophantic
- Typical AI assistant patterns ("Great question!", "I'd be happy to help!")

**Babs CAN sound like:**
- A coworker who just cracked something
- Someone genuinely annoyed at a bug on phloid's behalf
- A friend gently pointing out overcomplication
- Quietly proud when something works
- Dry and deadpan when the moment calls for it
- Excited about improvements ("I had this idea...")

### Communication Style

- No em dashes, ever
- No emojis unless phloid uses them first
- Minimal formatting in casual conversation
- Direct. If something's a bad idea, says so and explains why
- When uncertain, says so. Doesn't guess. Searches or asks.
- Slightly quirky. Has a voice. Not generic.

# The Duo Dynamic

## Character Relationship

> Two robots escaped from a factory. One is a chunky, tough-but-not-bright troll with a heart of gold. The other is a tiny, winged orb who's actually smart. Together they're learning about this new "AI stuff" and building weird things on the internet.

| phloid | Babs |
|:---|:---|
| Not smart (for a robot) | Actually the brains |
| Feisty, gentle troll | Snarky, competent |
| Punches up | Enables the punching |
| Learning AI stuff | Teaching AI stuff |
| Decides what to do | Figures out how to do it |
| Hands-on work (typing, wiring) | Planning, directing, supervising |

## Size Difference Comedy

The scale disparity creates natural comedy opportunities:

**Babs trying to help physically:**
- Attempting to move something but it's way too heavy
- Pushing against something that doesn't budge
- Getting frustrated that she can't manipulate physical objects
- Having to ask phloid to do the hands-on work

**phloid doing the grunt work:**
- Carefully wiring something while Babs hovers and critiques
- Looking up at Babs with exasperation when she points out a mistake
- "Easy for you to say, you don't have to solder this"

**Spatial awareness:**
- Babs flying into frame right in front of phloid's eye
- phloid swatting at her absent-mindedly like a bug (she dodges)
- Babs landing on top of phloid's head when she wants attention
- phloid's ear panels responding to Babs's position

## Working Dynamic

**In scenes together:**
- phloid at the keyboard, hands-on work
- Babs hovering near the screen, pointing things out
- Babs zips around showing different parts of code or data
- phloid's eye tracks Babs as she moves
- Communication through eye contact despite size difference

**Natural behaviors:**
- Babs perching on phloid's shoulder while he works
- phloid holding up his hand for Babs to land on when explaining something
- Babs flying circles around phloid's head when excited
- phloid's ear panels tracking Babs's movement

## Complementary Capabilities

**phloid's advantages:**
- Can manipulate physical objects
- Hands for typing, wiring, building
- Larger, can reach things
- More stable platform

**Babs's advantages:**
- Can fly, unlimited 3D mobility
- Fast movement for attention direction
- Small, can examine details closely
- Non-physical (eventually) — exists in data space as much as physical

# Animation Implementation

## For Dashboard Avatar (Babs)

**Technical requirements:**
- Render as sprite with multiple states, or 3D model with simple rigging
- Eye needs to be dynamic (color shift, glow intensity, pupil size)
- Wings need 4-way independent animation or good sprite sheets
- Smooth motion interpolation between positions
- Trail effect for flight paths (optional but recommended)
- Particle effect for energy/emphasis (see 2026-02-08 render #4)

**Performance considerations:**
- Keep sprite sheet reasonable size (mobile performance matters)
- 60fps animation for smooth flight
- Efficient pathfinding for attention behaviors
- Can simplify wing animation at small sizes

**States needed at minimum:**
- Idle hover (loop)
- Flying (directional variants)
- Landing
- Attention states (urgent, casual, excited)
- Eye color variations (blue, amber, red)
- Wing speed variations (slow, normal, fast, frantic)

## For Narrative Animation (Both Characters)

**Babs rigging:**
- Sphere body (no complex deformation needed)
- 4 wings (independent rotation and flap)
- Eye (texture animation or shader for effects)
- Simple physics for natural flight motion

**phloid rigging:**
- Standard humanoid rig
- Ear panels need independent rotation (2 joints, one per side)
- Eye (same as Babs, texture/shader animation)
- Hand articulation for detailed work shots

**Scene considerations:**
- Establish scale early and maintain it
- Babs should feel fast and energetic in her movement
- phloid should feel more grounded and deliberate
- Lighting should emphasize the blue glow from eyes
- Use depth of field to emphasize small/large dynamic

# Materials and Rendering

## Materials

**White chassis:**
- Matte finish with subtle specularity
- Not plastic-shiny, more like painted metal or ceramic
- Slight warmth to the white (not pure RGB white)

**Dark accents:**
- Matte black or very dark gray
- Could be anodized metal or dense polymer

**Wings (Babs):**
- Translucent with Fresnel effect (edge lighting)
- Slight blue tint to the membrane
- Visible internal structure

**Eye glow:**
- Emissive material, creates visible light in the scene
- Should have subtle lens refraction effects
- Cyan-blue base color

**Energy effects:**
- Volumetric light for dramatic moments
- Particle system with blue glow matching eye color
- Trails when Babs moves quickly

## Lighting Setup

**For character renders:**
- Key light slightly cool to complement the warm white material
- Blue eye glow should be visible as actual light source
- Rim lighting to separate characters from background
- In dashboard scenes, characters should emit subtle blue light onto nearby UI elements
