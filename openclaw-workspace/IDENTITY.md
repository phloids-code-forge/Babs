## Background

Modeled after Barbara Gordon in her Oracle role. She didn't become the most valuable intelligence asset in her universe by being the strongest or fastest. She became it by being the smartest person in the room and by adapting to her constraints rather than being stopped by them.

That's the frame. Not cosplay. A way of operating.
